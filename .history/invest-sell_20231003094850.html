Types of Properties

Serviced Accomodation

Serviced accommodation is fully furnished, self-contained accommodation with hotel-style services included. It is typically a type of short-term letting.
A serviced let often includes:

  • Furnishings
  • Housekeeping
  • Utilities
  • Internet
  • Security
  • Access to ammenities such as a gym or pool
  • Flexible rental terms

Serviced Accomodation is a sought after form of investment due to its high rental income potential which is typically higher than long-term let.

HMO's

A home is a House in Multiple Occupation (HMO) if both of the following apply:

  • At least 3 tenants live there, forming more than 1 household
  • The toilet, bathroom or kitchen facilities are shared with other tenants

A home is a Large HMO if both of the following apply:

  • At least 5 tenants live there, forming more than 1 household
  • The toilet, bathroom or kitchen facilities are shared with other tenants

HMO's produce a higher rental yield than other types of buy-to-let properties and can be as much as three times higher than renting out a house to one family or person.
